Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our services

What areas do you serve?
We serve the entire metropolitan area and surrounding suburbs. Contact us to confirm if we cover your specific location.
Do I need to provide cleaning supplies?
No! We bring all necessary equipment and eco-friendly cleaning products. If you have specific products you'd like us to use, we're happy to accommodate.
How long does a typical cleaning take?
It depends on the size of your space and the type of cleaning. A basic clean typically takes 2-3 hours, while deep cleaning can take 4-6 hours.
Are your cleaners insured and background-checked?
Absolutely! All our team members are fully insured, bonded, and undergo thorough background checks for your safety and peace of mind.
What is your cancellation policy?
We require 24 hours notice for cancellations. Cancellations made with less than 24 hours notice may incur a cancellation fee.
